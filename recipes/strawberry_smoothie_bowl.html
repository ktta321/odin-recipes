Strawberry Smoothie Bowl

Home

Description

A smoothe and refreshing bowl of blended strawberry packed with protein!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Frozen Strawberry
  • 1 serving Protein Powder
  • 3/4 cups Ice
  • 1/2 cups Low Fat Milk (any milk works)

Steps

  1. In a blender, add strawberries, protein powder, ice, and milk.
  2. Blend until thick and smoothe.
  3. Pour blend into bowl and enjoy! :D
