Steak Bites

Home

Description

Garlicy and buttery diced steak that is golden and cripsy!

Ingredients

  • 800g Raw Topside/Top Round Beef Steak
  • 1.5 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 tsp Parsley
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 2 tsp Olive Oil
  • 40g Light Butter
  • OPTIONAL: Minced Garlic, Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Slice your steak into cubes.
  2. Season with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, parsley, chilli powder, and olive oil.
  3. Mix until the color changes.
  4. Cook steak bites in light butter on med heat for 3 minutes on each side until golden and cripsy.
  5. OPTIONAL: Add more light butter, minced garlic, fresh parsley, and mix.
